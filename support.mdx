</Note>

## Contacting Support
For help, email [info@virtualcontrollersupply.com](mailto:info@virtualcontrollersupply.com). To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, please include:

1. The product name
2. Your operating system
3. A description of the issue
4. CRC logs, screenshots, or videos (if possible)
4. What troubleshooting steps you've tried and the results.

Once we receive your request, we'll review the information you provided and respond as appropriate.
