<Info>
  **System Requirements**

  - Windows 10 or 11 or macOS
  - CRC v2.9.0 or later
  - Available USB Port on your computer
</Info>

Follow these steps to get up and running with your STARS Keyboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plug it in">
    Connect the keyboard to your computer using the included USB cable, or supply your own if desired.\
    \
    Once plugged in, your computer will recognize the keyboard as a USB device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch CRC">
    v2.9.0 or later is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    \[NEED INSTRUCTIONS HERE\]
  </Step>
  <Step title="Press Any Key">
    Try pressing a few keys on the keyboard you should see:

    - An immediate response in CRC, for function keys
    - Text appearing in inputs fields, for text keys
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're Ready!">
    If key presses are registering, you're good to go.
  </Step>
</Steps>
