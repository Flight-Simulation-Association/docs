## We Love Feedback

Have an idea to improve one of our products?

Whether it’s:
- A feature request
- A usability improvement
- A hardware idea
- Documentation feedback

—we’d love to hear it!

## What to Include

To help us understand your feedback, try to include:

- Which product you're referring to
- What you were trying to do
- What could be improved
- Your suggestion or idea
- Why/how it would help your workflow

### Examples

- “It would be helpful if [product] supported ___”
- “The layout of [product] could be improved by ___”
- “I expected ___ but got ___”
- “This part of the documentation could be clearer: ___”

## Submitting Feedback

Please email your feedback directly to [info@virtualcontrollersupply.com](mailto:info@virtualcontrollersupply.com).
